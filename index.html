Not Your Average Engineers

We don't just build things, we solve human problems. We're Thomas and Caitria, a front-end/back-end contract team that builds beautiful experiences for web and mobile. We're perfect for your research, design and engineering projects.

Design Process

Let's work together! Here's a look at our design and engineering processes. If you can use help in any of the following areas, let's get together and discuss your goals and timeline.


1. Needfinding & User Research

We use human-centered design and methods pioneered at Stanford's d.school to get to know your users and the problems they face. A successful research sprint culminates in a clear strategy and scope for future work.


2. Concept Design & Prototyping

Based upon our understanding of your business goals and user needs, we quickly design and prototype solutions to work toward a final set of designs for the function, architecture, and visual interface of your product.


3. Engineering, Documentation & Support

We build your big idea, turning a successful prototype into a functional, scalable and marketable product. Working with your team, we choose technologies that make sense for the task and work for your team. We document beautifully and hand off smoothly.
