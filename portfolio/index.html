Recovers.org - community disaster recovery

{% image recovers_screenshot.png %}

Prior to joining Pocketknife, Caitria was the co-founder and CEO of Recovers.org, an award winning disaster relief platform. Inspired by a tornado in her hometown, Monson, MA, she developed tools to help neighbors handle the resources that pour into their communities after disasters. There's no better way to create software than on the ground with your users - Caitria spent weeks in disaster areas working with survivors to co-design solutions to the problems they faced.

{% image recovers_screenshot2.png %}

Keeping respect for users at the core of all research and development led to software that has helped hundreds of thousands of people get involved in local recovery. Caitria received a Champion of Change award from the White House for her contributions to the field of disaster recovery.
